Tiny and Copper

I made this webpage to talk about my two great pups. I have two dogs, Copper and Tiny. Tiny is a six year old Shih Tzu Chihuahua mix. Copper is an eleven year old Golden Retriever. Copper and Tiny both live at my parents' house. I miss them a lot while I am at school. They are so sweet and obviously photogenic! Copper was a gift to me and my sisters from my parents for Christmas one year. We received him and his sister, Ginger. She passed away a few years ago. They were tiny little pups with red bows on them. The joy of receiving them was so much fun! Tiny was a stray dog that my aunt found and my family and I loved him so much immediately she gave him to us. I'm so glad to have them both now!
